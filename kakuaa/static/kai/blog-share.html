In order to receive the best answers to your questions please follow these simple guidelines.

  1. Be descriptive about your question or problem and be sure to include any errors. Single line topics will rarely receive answers.

  2. Provide examples of the code in question and use syntax highlighting. Just enclose your code in 3 backticks ( ``` ), like this: ``` document.getElementById("main") ``` .

  3. Breakdown the issue in your code and provide a link to an example that replicates it. Use JSFiddle, CodePen, JSBin, or CodeSandbox

Following these few steps will greatly improve your chances of getting a good quality answer to your issue. Remember, everyone is here to share ideas and to help out using their own free time. Making an effort to craft a well thought out topic pays tribute to this.
